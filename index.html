Seekers Alliance is a multiplayer trading card game (TCG) developed with blockchain technology. Its three major Factions each offer cards with distinct characteristics and playstyles for players to assemble into decks.

Through PvP card battles, players can compete for each other’s valuable cards within the Island’s regions. The first player to collect all 100 Bounty Cards of the Season wins the Season Championship and becomes the Ruler of the Seekers Alliance game world.
Adventure Mode
The Island’s diverse regions are teeming with dangerous mobs. Bring your deck & spell cards as you face difficult challenges – the rewards will surely be worth your while.

But keep an eye out, for other Seekers may be eyeing your collection!

Ladder Mode
Opting for a quick battle?
Try Ladder Mode.

Compete against other players for Ladder Rankings to win weekly prizes and limited-edition cards without ever losing cards to your opponents.
Bounty Cards
Collect 100 designated Bounty Cards by opening card packs, battling other players, and exploring the Island.

Be the first to stake them into the Card Binder to win the Season Championship, along with generous prizes and a decisive advantage for the next Season.

Tournaments are where you’ll find the toughest opponents and the highest-rarity cards.

Various formats, battlefields, and themes will be selected to ensure that all player types get an opportunity under the spotlight.
